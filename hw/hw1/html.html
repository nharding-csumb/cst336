C++ and Python are used for performing specific computational tasks, but that's not all computers are helpful for. Sometimes you don't need to calculate something, you just need to make a webpage with some kind of structure. That's were HTML comes in.

HTML is a programming language designed speficially for webpages. It allows someone to give structure and form to a page, to make it easier for visitors to the site to navigate the site and use it for its intended purpose, whatever that may be.

HTML is slightly more text-heavy than languages like C++ and Python, but still maintans ease of readability due to its reliance on a system of tags to define what is being displayed.
